How to Play

Wizard Duel is a 1v1 game. Two wizards fight in a small arena with trees for cover.

Controls

WASD — Move

1 + Left Click — Cast Blood Red (high damage, slow)

2 + Left Click — Cast Dark Blue (low damage, fast)

Right Click — Heal with mana shield

Z (hold) — Zoom out, regenerate mana faster

Spells
Blood Red

35 damage. Medium speed. Large radius. Your heavy hitter.

Dark Blue

25 damage. High speed. Small radius. For precise shots.

Mana Shield

Right click to heal. Costs 10 mana, restores 2 HP. Use wisely.

Setup

1. Create an account to get your API key.

2. Put the key in a file called wizard_api_key.txt next to the game.

3. Launch the game and start dueling.
