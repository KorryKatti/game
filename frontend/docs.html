SPELLBOOK

CORE MECHANICS

Wizard Duel is a real-time tactical dueling platform. Victory is determined by energy management, spell accuracy, and positioning.

1. MOVEMENT & POSITIONING

Combat occurs on a grid-based arena. Positioning affects spell travel time and line-of-sight.

2. SPELL CASTING

Every spell costs energy and has a cooldown. Chaining spells requires precise timing to avoid depletion.

3. RANKING SYSTEM

ELO calculates skill based on match outcomes. Defeating higher-ranked opponents yields greater gains.

SPELL ARSENAL

  • FIREBALL
    High damage, medium speed. Primary offensive tool.
  • FROST BOLT
    Low damage, slows target. Strategic utility.
  • ARCANE SHIELD
    Negates incoming damage for 1.5 seconds.
